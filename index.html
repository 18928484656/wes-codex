Buyer questions

Common questions before quotation.

What information is needed for a fast LED display quote?

Please share the screen size, pixel pitch, indoor or outdoor use, installation location, application, quantity and destination country.

Do you support OEM or ODM orders?

Yes. LEDUSS supports OEM/ODM customization for importers, contractors and project buyers when the project requirements are clear.

Which LED display product should I choose?

Indoor projects usually need fine pixel pitch and high refresh rate. Outdoor projects need higher brightness and weather protection. Rental projects often need lightweight cabinets and fast installation.
